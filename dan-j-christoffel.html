Dan J. Christoffel

Dan J. Christoffel, Ph.D.

PI of Christoffel-lab-UNC at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill (Psychology and Neuroscience Department); Assistant Professor in the Behavioral and Integrative Neuroscience Program. Spouse of Jessica-J-Walsh (PI of Walsh-lab-UNC). Postdoc training with Robert-C-Malenka at Stanford; PhD training in the Russo-lab-Mount-Sinai.
